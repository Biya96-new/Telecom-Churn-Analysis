# 📊 Telecom Churn Analysis – Business Intelligence Dashboard  

## 🏢 Business Context  
Customer churn is a critical challenge in the telecom industry, directly impacting revenue and long-term growth.  

This project analyzes customer data to identify patterns behind churn across plans, contract types, regions, and usage behavior—helping businesses take proactive steps to improve retention.  

---

## 🎯 Objectives  
- Evaluate overall customer churn rate  
- Compare churn across subscription plans and contract types  
- Analyze the impact of customer usage on churn  
- Identify high-risk customer segments  
- Provide actionable recommendations to improve retention  

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack  
**Power BI | Power Query | DAX | Excel**  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Overview  
The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of churn through:  

- **Overview →** Total customers, churned customers, churn rate  
- **Plan Analysis →** Churn comparison across Basic, Standard, Premium  
- **Contract Analysis →** Monthly vs Annual churn behavior  
- **Regional Analysis →** Churn distribution across regions  
- **Usage Analysis →** Impact of data usage (Low vs High)  
- **Risk Segmentation →** High-risk combinations (Plan + Contract + Usage)  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics  
- Total Customers  
- Churn Rate  
- Churn by Plan  
- Churn by Contract Type  
- Churn by Region  
- Churn by Usage  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights  
- **11.22% of customers churned (101 out of 900)**  
- **Premium plan recorded the highest churn rate (14.21%)**  
- **Monthly contracts had higher churn (14.29%) vs Annual (8.09%)**  
- **East region had the highest churn (12.99%)**, followed by North (11.32%)  
- **Low usage customers showed the highest churn (16.28%)**  
- **Annual plans consistently showed lower churn across segments**  
- **Lowest churn observed in Standard + Annual + High Usage (4.08%)**  

---

## 🚨 Critical High-Risk Segment  
- **Basic + Monthly + Low Usage → 28.57% churn**  

👉 This segment represents the most vulnerable customer group due to low engagement and low commitment  

---

## 💡 Recommendations  

- Introduce **discounted annual plans** to reduce churn from monthly users  
- Provide **limited-time upgrade offers** to encourage long-term commitment  
- Clearly communicate **premium features and benefits** to improve value perception  
- Offer **personalized recommendations** to increase customer usage  

📍 **For high churn regions (East & North):**  
- Investigate potential **network/service quality issues**  
- Run **region-specific campaigns and offers**  

---

## 🚀 Business Value  
- Delivered clear visibility into customer churn patterns  
- Identified high-risk segments requiring immediate attention  
- Enabled data-driven retention strategies  
- Transformed raw customer data into an interactive dashboard
